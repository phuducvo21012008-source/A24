About A24

A24 is an independent entertainment company known for bold, creator-driven stories that push boundaries in film and television. Founded in 2012, the studio has built a distinctive brand by championing visionary filmmakers and unique perspectives. Its catalog includes critically acclaimed titles like Moonlight, Hereditary, Lady Bird, The Lighthouse, and Everything Everywhere All at Once. From art-house horror to emotional coming-of-age dramas, A24 has become synonymous with innovative storytelling and cinematic risk-taking.
